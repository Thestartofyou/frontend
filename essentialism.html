About Us

“optimizing what we keep in our lives in the pursuit of doing better.” Essentialism at home is creating an environment that’s supportive of the life you want to live. Often that means simplifying, but it doesn’t necessarily mean minimizing. It means having around you what you need (and only what you need) to be highly functional, effective, peaceful and comfortable so that you can thrive.
